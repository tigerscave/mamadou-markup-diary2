Typography

While typography has been practiced for many centuries, digital font design is a
relatively new discipline. There are some great examples of old-school fonts (also called typefaces) which have been adopted for use on digital displays.
However, i would like to highlight a few of my favorite fonts that were created
with screens in mind.

Serif

Serifs are the small features at the end of sttrokes within letters. These features are Functional as well as decorative.
Serif fonts are widely used for body text (eg. artcles) because they are cosidered they are considered
easier to read than sans-serif fonts in print.

serif fonts can often create a feeling of traditionalism and antiquity,
due to their common use in printed materials for many years.
